# BuybackAI — Outreach playbook

Live: https://buybackai.vercel.app

## Positioning (one-liner)
BuybackAI is a marketplace of focused AI agents that take $20-an-hour work off founders' plates. Audit your week, hire an agent, pay only while it works.

## ICP — first 50 conversations
1. Solo / 2-person SaaS founders, $5k–$50k MRR, drowning in inbox + ops.
2. Agency owners (3–15 people) doing client comms themselves.
3. Service-business operators (HVAC, legal, fitness, real estate) — the "boring market" angle. Fewer SaaS bros, higher willingness to pay for time back.

## Channels (in order of expected ROI)
1. Personal warm intros — DM founder friends, ask for 5 min.
2. Indie Hackers + r/SaaS launch posts.
3. X — launch thread + reply guy in #BuildInPublic.
4. LinkedIn — post + DM founders in HVAC / agency / fitness niches.
5. Communities: SaaS Mastermind, MicroConf, Dan Martell's Buyback community, Hampton.
6. Cold email — list of 200 founders, see `cold-email.md`.

## Asks
Stage 1 (this week): 30 free audits. Goal = 30 calls = signal. No payment.
Stage 2 (next week): convert 10 of 30 to a paid agent at $19–29/mo.
