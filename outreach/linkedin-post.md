# LinkedIn launch post

I shipped a thing today.

It's called BuybackAI.

The premise is simple: most founders aren't starved for tools. They're drowning in $20-an-hour work wearing a CEO's hat.

So I built a marketplace where founders hire AI agents the way they'd hire a contractor.

→ Connect your Gmail and Calendar (read-only)
→ Get a 2-minute audit that shows — in dollars, at your hourly rate — exactly what hours an agent could be doing for you
→ Hire the agent that fits
→ Pay only while it's deployed

Three agents are live at launch:

• Inbox Ivy — $29/mo. Sorts your email by urgency and drafts replies in your voice.
• Meeting Marv — $19/mo. Five minutes before every call: a one-page brief on the person, the company, and three things worth raising.
• Follow-up Fred — $19/mo. Watches your sent folder. When a thread goes quiet, it drafts the nudge.

I'm running 30 free audits this week. No card. No pitch. I just want to see if the picture matches what founders think their week looks like.

If you want one, comment "audit" or DM me and I'll send the link.

(Built solo. Claude + Supabase + Next.js + Vercel. Live at buybackai.vercel.app)
