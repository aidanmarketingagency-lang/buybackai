# LinkedIn profile shift — BuybackAI founder positioning

The strategy: lead with **"non-technical founder who shipped a real AI product solo"** — this is the hook. Then back it up with the marketing-agency past so you don't read as a hobbyist. The profile should make a stranger think "I want to follow this person" within 3 seconds of landing.

Edit each section below to match your voice, then paste into LinkedIn.

---

## 1. Profile photo

Keep your existing one if it's a clean headshot, neutral background, looking at camera. If it's old or busy:
- Plain background (off-white, charcoal, or studio gray)
- Shoulders-up, eyes facing camera
- Slight smile, not corporate stiff

Don't use a logo as your profile pic — LinkedIn punishes that algorithmically.

## 2. Banner image (the 1584×396 strip)

Use `public/brand/banner.png` from the repo (already on-brand: dark BG, accent green, "BuybackAI" mark). Upload directly.

## 10. The first post — make the shift visible

Once the profile is updated, make the change visible with a launch post. Use `outreach/linkedin-post.md` (already drafted in this repo). Post it within 24h of the profile change so your network sees both at once.

After that: aim for 2 posts/week, mostly about what you're building / learnings. The "build in public" cadence drives the most warm DMs.

## 11. Cleanup quick wins

- **Settings → Open to** — turn ON "Providing services" → list "AI Consulting" or similar so the green "Open to" badge shows on your profile
- **Settings → Profile information** → make sure your industry is **Software Development** or **Internet** (not "Marketing & Advertising"). LinkedIn shows different reactions based on industry.
- **Mute or filter old agency posts** if they don't fit the new positioning. Don't delete — that looks panicked. Just stop boosting.
